# Alphabetical Exhibit Ordering

## Overview

Ensure FairBook presents exhibition departments in alphabetical order.

## Requirements

- Place Antlers before Art & Antiques in the `departments` data.
- Keep the public exhibits list alphabetized by displayed department name.
- Keep derived department downloads in the same alphabetical order.
- Add an automated test that verifies alphabetical department ordering.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Antlers is the first exhibit shown.
- Every subsequent exhibit is alphabetically ordered by name.
- Department downloads follow the same order.
- The relevant test suite passes.

## Out of Scope

- Changing department names, content, or classes.
- Reordering any non-department content.
