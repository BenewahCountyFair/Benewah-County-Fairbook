# Product Guide

## Purpose

FairBook is a clear, searchable public guide to fair exhibitions, rules, and entry classes.

## Primary Audience

Fair visitors browsing events and exhibits.

## Content Principles

- Preserve the official fairbook as the authoritative source.
- Make content straightforward to update when rules or schedules change.
- Cover exhibition classes, departments, and entry rules.

## Content Maintenance

A technical maintainer updates source files from official fair documents and preserves the accuracy of the published guide.
