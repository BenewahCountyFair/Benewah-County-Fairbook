# Product Guidelines

## Content Presentation

- Use plain, scannable language with prominent class titles and numbered entry lists.
- Preserve official rule and judging-criteria wording, organized into readable paragraphs.
- Keep a clear hierarchy between departments, classes, rules, and entries.

## Consistency

- Apply consistent spacing and formatting across all exhibition pages.
- Within each class, use a single sequential numbered list beginning at 1; never reset numbering partway through a class.
- Provide prominent links to printable official resources where relevant.

## Accessibility and Usability

- Use semantic headings and lists for screen-reader navigation.
- Maintain a responsive, readable layout on phones and desktops.
